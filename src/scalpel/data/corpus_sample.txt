# One snippet per line; blank lines and lines starting with '#' are ignored.

# --- dogs ---
The dog ran across the yard chasing after the red rubber ball.

The golden retriever wagged its tail and licked the child's face.
We took the dog for a long walk along the muddy riverside trail.

# --- cats ---
The cat curled up on the windowsill and watched the birds outside.
A cat will knock a glass off the table just to see what happens.
Our kitten pounced on the ball of yarn and rolled across the floor.
The old tabby cat purred softly as it dozed by the fireplace.
Cats spend most of the day sleeping and grooming their soft fur.

# --- weather ---
Dark storm clouds gathered over the hills before the heavy rain fell.
The weather forecast promised sunshine but delivered a cold drizzle.
A gentle breeze carried the smell of rain across the open fields.
Thunder rumbled in the distance as lightning split the night sky.
It was a bright, warm afternoon with barely a cloud in the sky.

# --- ocean ---
The waves crashed against the rocks and sprayed foam into the air.
Deep in the ocean, strange fish glow in the cold and lightless water.
Sailors watched the horizon as the tide pulled their small boat out.
The coral reef teemed with colourful fish darting between the rocks.
Salt spray stung our faces as the ship rolled over the grey swell.

# --- music ---

The song's chorus stayed stuck in my head for the rest of the day.

# --- food ---

# --- emotions ---
